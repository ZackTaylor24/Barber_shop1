Welcome !

Welcome to my website. On this site I will tell you all about my Barber Shop and also about about myself and how I got into hairdressing. I have now been a barber for over 4 years. I learnt how to cut hair at the Lycée des métiers des Arts de la Coiffure in Lyon. In addition to school i worked in a small barber shop in Croix Rousse. I passed my CAP and Brevet professionel and also participated in a week long training course in Holland at the Schorem Barber school from which I was granted a certificat.
If you have any questions about getting a haircut or about hairdressing in general feel free to send me a message. You will find all my contact information on this page ! Looking forward to having a chat !

Who is Zack ?

Born in England, I moved to france at a very young age. I have now been living in France for over 15 years and in Lyon for the past 9 years. I have also lived in Dublin for a year and enjoy travelling very much. I am 26 years old and have many countries that I would still like to visit.

Like I said previously, If you have any questions at all feel free to contact me. You can find all my contact info on this page. Until then, peace out !
